学校简介

School Introduction

新疆生产建设兵团第二中学（简称"兵团二中"）创建于1951年，是新疆生产建设兵团直属的重点中学，自治区示范性普通高级中学。学校位于乌鲁木齐市天山区，占地面积85亩，建筑面积5万余平方米。

Founded in 1951, Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps No.2 Middle School (referred to as "Bingtuan No.2 Middle School") is a key middle school directly under the Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps and a demonstration ordinary senior high school in the autonomous region. The school is located in Tianshan District, Urumqi, covering an area of 85 mu with a construction area of more than 50,000 square meters.

学校核定编制总数为350人，核定教学班78个（小学24个、初中18个、高中36个）。近三年，学校教师共有50余人次获省级、国家级讲课大赛一等奖，在省级以上期刊发表论文一百二十多篇。截至2015年9月，学校有国务院特殊津贴专家2人、“兵团英才工程”第一层次人才2人、自治区特级教师9人。

The school has an approved total staff of 350 people, with 78 approved teaching classes (24 in primary school, 18 in junior high, and 36 in senior high). In the past three years, over 50 teachers from the school have won first prizes in provincial and national teaching competitions, and more than 120 papers have been published in provincial-level and higher journals. As of September 2015, the school has 2 experts receiving special allowances from the State Council, 2 first-level talents from the 'Corps Talent Project,' and 9 teachers with special-grade status in the autonomous region".

学校秉承"自强不息，攀高不止"的办学理念，坚持素质教育，注重学生全面发展。多年来，学校教育教学质量稳步提升，高考成绩在全疆名列前茅，为国家和社会培养了大批优秀人才。

Adhering to the educational philosophy of "Strive unceasingly for self-improvement, and keep climbing to greater heights", the school insists on quality education and focuses on the all-round development of students. Over the years, the school's teaching quality has steadily improved, and its college entrance examination results rank among the top in Xinjiang, cultivating a large number of outstanding talents for the country and society.

近些年学校先后被评为“全国教育系统先进集体”、“全国学校及周边环境综合治理先进单位”、“全国平安和谐校园”、“全国消防安全教育示范学校”、“全国文明单位”等称号，得到了社会的广泛认可和好评。

In recent years, the school has successively been awarded titles such as 'National Advanced Collective in the Education System,' 'National Advanced Unit for Comprehensive Management of Schools and Surrounding Environments,' 'National Safe and Harmonious Campus,' 'National Demonstration School for Fire Safety Education,' and 'National Civilized Unit,' receiving widespread recognition and praise from society.

学校历史

School History

1951

建校初期

学校前身是中国人民解放军二十二兵团后勤运输部子弟学校，创建于1951年，是新疆最早的汉语学校之一。

The predecessor of the school was the Children's School of the Logistics and Transportation Department of the 22nd Corps of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, founded in 1951, and was one of the earliest Chinese schools in Xinjiang.

1959

蓬勃发展

发展成为新疆维吾尔自治区首批公立重点中学，开始进入快速发展时期。

Developed into one of the first public key high schools in Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, and began to enter a period of rapid development.

2006

示范高中

被评为"兵团示范性普通高级中学"，教学质量和办学水平位居全疆前列。

Rated as a 'Model General Senior High School of the Corps', its teaching quality and school management level are among the top in the whole Xinjiang.
